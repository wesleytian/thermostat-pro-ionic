Short cycle is the minimum duration that the system has to rest before being turned on again to reduce damage to the compressor. Many systems have the recommended short cycle duration written on it somewhere.
Use Heat Index
System hysterisis is the temperature difference between the turn-on and turn-off. A narrower range means a more stable room temperature while a larger range can mean less power consumption. (A temperature set at 75° with a 1° range means the system will turn on when it is 75.5° and turn off when it is 74.5°.)
